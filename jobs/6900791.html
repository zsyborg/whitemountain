Corporate Accounting Intern (Remote)

A24 is seeking an intern to support the Corporate Accounting team -- minimum availability of 15 hours total per week, preferably on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays.

This internship is remote, and we're open to candidates from any location in the US. 

Responsibilities

  • Gather documents requested by auditors (e.g., license agreements, vendor royalty statements, etc.)
  • Verify that supporting documents align with amounts reported in the General Ledger
  • Assist with timely communication and responses to auditors
  • Update and maintain audit trackers to reflect the current status and progress of each audit

Qualifications

  • Experience with financial documents
  • Attention to Detail: Ability to accurately review and match supporting documents with financial records
  • Organizational Skills: Capable of managing multiple audit requests and maintaining detailed trackers
  • Communication Skills: Strong written and verbal communication skills to liaise with internal teams and external auditors
  • Analytical Thinking: Basic understanding of financial statements and general ledger reconciliations
  • Time Management: Able to prioritize tasks and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment
  • Technical Proficiency: Familiarity with Microsoft Excel and general office software
  • Discretion: Ability to handle confidential and sensitive financial information with professionalism

We are looking for diverse perspectives. A24 is an Equal Opportunity Employer and encourages applications from all kinds of candidates.

Our internships are part-time roles with an hourly rate of $18.00. 

A24 is an acclaimed global entertainment company known for producing and distributing films, television series, documentaries, music, publishing, and consumer products. Established in New York in 2012, A24 has cultivated a library of over 150 films and 50 TV series, earning critical and commercial acclaim with more than 21 Oscars, 18 Golden Globes, and 18 Emmys. Renowned for groundbreaking hits like Moonlight, Everything Everywhere All at Once, and Uncut Gems, as well as television successes such as Euphoria, Beef, and Ramy, A24 has developed a passionate fanbase and expanded into ventures like Half Magic and the Cherry Lane theater. Upcoming projects include Marty Supreme starring Timothée Chalamet and Gwyneth Paltrow, The Drama starring Zendaya and Robert Pattinson, The Smashing Machine starring Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson and Emily Blunt, Materialists starring Chris Evans, Pedro Pascal and Dakota Johnson, and Eddington starring Joaquin Phoenix, Pedro Pascal, and Emma Stone, among others.
